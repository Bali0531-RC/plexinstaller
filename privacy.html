Overview

PlexDev.live is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

PlexDev.live does not collect any personal information from visitors. Our website serves purely as a documentation and download portal for installation scripts.

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, some information may be automatically collected through third-party services:

  • Google AdSense: May collect anonymous data such as IP addresses, browser types, and general location for advertising purposes
  • Web Server Logs: Standard server logs that may include IP addresses, user agents, and access times for security and maintenance purposes

How We Use Information

The limited information that may be collected is used for:

  • Displaying relevant advertisements through Google AdSense
  • Maintaining website security and performance
  • Analyzing general website usage patterns (anonymized)

We do not:

  • Store personal information on our servers
  • Track individual users
  • Sell or share any data with third parties (except as required by Google AdSense)
  • Require user registration or accounts

Third-Party Services

Google AdSense

This website uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense may use cookies and web beacons to collect information about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.

For more information about Google's privacy practices, please visit: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Installation Scripts

When you download and execute our installation scripts, they operate entirely on your server. We do not receive any information about your server configuration, installed software, or usage patterns.

Cookies

PlexDev.live itself does not set any cookies. However, Google AdSense may set cookies or use local storage for advertising purposes, subject to your consent and regional requirements. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

To opt out of personalized advertising, you can visit: Google Ad Settings

EEA/UK Consent

We implement Google’s Consent Mode v2. In the European Economic Area and the United Kingdom, ad storage, ad user data, ad personalization, and analytics storage are set to “denied” by default and only change if consent is obtained via a consent management platform (CMP). Until consent is granted, only limited, non-personalized behavior may occur.

Outside the EEA/UK, standard AdSense behavior applies. You can change your preferences at any time using your browser controls and Google’s ad settings linked above.

Data Security

Since we do not collect or store personal information, there are no personal data security concerns related to our website directly. However, we implement standard security measures to protect our website infrastructure.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this privacy policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Information
